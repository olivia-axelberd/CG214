Sally Mann

One photographer who is influential to me creatively is Sally Mann. Sally Mann was born in Virginia in 1951 and is one of America's most well known photographers. She has won many awards and in 2001 she was named "America's Best Photographer" by Time magazine. She is most well known for photographing her family and for her images of her children.

Mann's Books

These are a few of her books in print:

  • At Twelve
  • Still Time
  • Immediate Family
  • What Remains

Her most recent book is a memoir titled Hold Still which came out in 2015. Hold Still quickly became a New York Times bestseller.
